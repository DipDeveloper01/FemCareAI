PCOS:
Symptoms:
- irregular periods
- acne
- weight gain
- hair loss
- missed periods

Description:
A hormonal disorder that commonly affects women of reproductive age.

--------------------------------------------------

Endometriosis:
Symptoms:
- pelvic pain
- painful periods
- pain during intercourse
- heavy menstrual bleeding

Description:
A condition where tissue similar to the uterine lining grows outside the uterus.

--------------------------------------------------

Uterine Fibroids:
Symptoms:
- heavy bleeding
- pelvic pressure
- frequent urination
- lower back pain

Description:
Non-cancerous growths in the uterus.

--------------------------------------------------

Ovarian Cysts:
Symptoms:
- pelvic pain
- bloating
- abdominal discomfort

Description:
Fluid-filled sacs that develop on the ovaries.

--------------------------------------------------

Pregnancy:
Symptoms:
- missed period
- nausea
- breast tenderness
- fatigue

Description:
The state of carrying a developing embryo or fetus.


Breast Pain Syndrome:
Symptoms:
- mastalgia
- breast pain
- nipple tenderness
- hard nipples

Description:
Breast pain or nipple tenderness that may be associated with hormonal changes.
